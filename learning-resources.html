LEARNING RESOURCES

Amberbrook Toys is the exclusive distributor for Learning Resources in Malaysia.

Learning Resources is the leading global manufacturer of educational resources which are trusted by teachers and parents and loved by children. Their high-quality school supplies are sold in more than 80 countries and they've won many awards for their innovative products. They make education fun for children, teachers and parents and their products are suitable for use in both the home and in classrooms!

Learning Resources works closely with teachers to design their products which are up-to-date with the latest curricula. They are a leader in STEM (Science Technology Engineering Maths) and SEN (Special Educational Needs) toys.
