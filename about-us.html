About Us

Amberbrook Toys distributes high quality, premium toys and games and educational products in Malaysia.

We represent companies from the UK, Europe and the US which meet the highest international standards for quality, safety and reliability.

Amberbrook Toys is the exclusive distributor in Malaysia for Djeco, Little Big Room by Djeco, BrainBox Games and The Green Board Game Company.

We're also the official distributor in Malaysia for Educational Insights and Learning Resources, acknowledged leaders in the field of educational toys and school resources.

You'll find our products in the leading toy stores across Malaysia.
