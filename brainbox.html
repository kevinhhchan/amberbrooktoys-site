THE GREEN BOARD GAME CO.

The Green Board Game Co. makes innovative and imaginative games to bring people together, increase knowledge, aid learning and be fun to play! They offer strategy games, card games, Lotto, Snap and Pairs and the award-winning BrainBox.

BrainBox is a series of fast-paced visual-memory games covering a range of themes and topics. You'll find facts about the world, languages, sport, numbers and science, as well as famous books from Roald Dahl to Horrible Histories, Shakespeare to Mr Men!

The Green Board Game Co. uses recycled / recyclable materials and sources these ethically. Amberbrook Toys is proud to be the exclusive distributor in Malaysia for BrainBox Games and The Green Board Game Co.
