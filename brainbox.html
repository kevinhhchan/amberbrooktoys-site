THE GREEN BOARD GAME CO.

The Green Board Game Co. makes innovative and imaginative games designed to bring people together, increase knowledge, aid learning and be fun to play!

They offer a variety of strategy games, card games, Lotto, Snap and Pairs and the award-winning BrainBox visual-memory game.

BrainBox games cover a range of themes and topics. You'll find facts about the world, languages, sport, numbers and science, as well as famous books from Roald Dahl to Horrible Histories, Shakespeare to Mr Men!

The Green Board Game Co. always uses as high a content of recycled / recyclable material as possible and sources their materials ethically.
