DJECO

Amberbrook Toys is proud to be the exclusive distributor for Djeco in Malaysia. Based in Paris, France, Djeco creates quality toys with French flair! You'll find a spectacular line of imaginative, award-winning toys including puzzles, wooden toys, role play sets, games, building blocks and arts and crafts kits.

Djeco commissions talented artists and designers to create their products, using vibrant colours and intricate illustrations.

You'll find their beautifully-designed products in Malaysia's best toy stores. Djeco can be found even prestigious museums including the Louvre and Quai Branly in France, the Tate in the UK, and MoMA in the USA!
