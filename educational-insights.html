EDUCATIONAL INSIGHTS

Educational Insights has been helping kids learn through play for more than 50 years. They make hands-on educational products, targeting nearly every school subject for students in kindergarten through eighth grade.

Based in sunny California in the USA, their products include electronic learning aids, activity books, science and nature kits, board games and many products for use in schools and homes. They take great pride in working with experts in many distinct fields- from scientists to teachers to artists- to ensure their products are always the very best they can be!

Amberbrook Toys is the exclusive distributor in Malaysia for Educational Insights.
